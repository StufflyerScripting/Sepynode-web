About Sepynode

Sepynode is a powerful and friendly programming language developed for learning and building cool stuff.

Why use Sepynode?

  • Fast, easy to learn
  • Free, open source
  • Wide, expandable
  • Huge friendly Dev Community :)

    • The Story of Sepynode

    Sepynode was originally created in July 2023 by SpottedZulu, a teenager. Back then the compiler was written in Python, after ImStufflyer, another teenager joining the project, the compiler was completly rewritten in C++ for optimization and speed.
